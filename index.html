ABOUT ME

My name is Colbert Tse, and I am a Bay Area Native who calls San Francisco my home. Having always been facinated by technology and computers, I am taking FEWD, General Assembly's course on Front End Web Design to learn and build upon my knowledge working with the web. In addition to working with technology, I am a foodie who is fond of the great food scene that the Bay Area has to offer. One of my favorite places is Lers Ros for Thai food. Also, I enjoy biking in San Francisco to get around town and being active in the bicycle scene as a member of the San Francisco Bicycle Coalition. Also, I enjoy music a lot whether it is going to concerts at great venues like the Fillmore to joy of listening to music on vinyl.

I look forward to expanding my basic skills in HTML to advancing them as I learn HTML 5, CSS, and Javascript, the fundamentals of Front-End Web Development.
